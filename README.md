## Core Case Study Insights

### 1. Operations Turnaround (SLA Control)
The initial Seller Central baseline exposed **139 unshipped FBM orders** (fulfillment suspension hazard) and **2 customer messages exceeding 24 hours**. 
- *Action*: Implemented a strict 24-hour customer messaging SLA and deployed the FBM daily Priority packing list tracker (SOP-OPS-001).
- *Outcome*: 100% of the shipping backlog cleared, stabilizing account health, and recovering seller feedback from **3.60 to 3.72 / 5 stars** in under 60 days.

### 2. Catalog & Listing SEO Optimization
Auditing the top volume driver (ASIN: B0BS6QM2GD) revealed 5,326 views but only 98 orders—translating to a weak **1.84% conversion rate**.
- *Action*: Overhauled the product listing (Title, Bullet Points, and Backend Search Terms) via automated AI optimization prompts, and drafted an FBA conversion roadmap to enroll in Prime.
- *Outcome*: Projected conversion rate scaling to **4.1%**, boosting catalog cash flow and clearing FBM warehouse space.

### 3. PPC Ads Efficiency
Campaign data showed 2.15M impressions, 23.5K clicks, CPC of ₹2.75, ad spend of ₹64.7K, and an ACoS of 29.3%.
- *Action*: Pruned low-converting keywords, optimized bidding caps, and focused budgets on high-converting product categories.
- *Outcome*: Reduced campaign ACoS to **23.3%** and increased ROAS multiplier to **4.25x**.

### 4. Agile Team Delegation (Hiring plan)
- *Action*: Built a 4-sprint agile roadmap in Jira to allocate tasks (AMZ-OPT-60D).
- *Outcome*: Hired and onboarded two virtual assistants—a Logistics VA to run the daily shipping queue, and a Support VA to guarantee response SLAs—scaling operations to free up executive strategy bandwidth.
